Add Gate Drawings: parametric to-scale slide gate elevation generator

New "Gate Drawings" tab in the app window. Enter clear opening, gate
height, post size and drive type; a live SVG preview redraws as you type
and "Save drawing PDF" writes a true vector, to-scale PDF.

- drawgen.py computes the elevation once as primitive ops (lines/rects/
  text) in PDF points, rendered identically as SVG (preview) and PDF
  (save) so they always match. Auto-fits a standard architectural scale
  (1" .. 1/16" = 1'-0") and labels it; dimensioned clear opening + height,
  posts, ground line, title block.
- app.py: /api/drawing/preview (SVG+scale), /api/drawing/save (PDF to a
  drawings/ folder), /download-drawing; desktop.py opens saved drawings in
  the default viewer (auto-opens on save), browser falls back to download.
- drawings/ gitignored (work products).

v1 is the simple schematic; next: cantilever tail, infill/bracing, then
electrical drawings.
